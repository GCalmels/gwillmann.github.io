Don't know if it is a bug or if it is intended by Apple.

In order to restore a specific folder/file from a specific time :

  • Enter TimeMachine
  • Select your computer hard drive in the bar on the left
  • Select the specific time that you want to restore
  • Go to the folder you want to restore
  • Copy & Paste to restore
